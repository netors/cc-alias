This script itself does not collect any data. However, when configured with OpenTelemetry:

  • Telemetry data may be sent to the configured OTEL endpoint
  • This may include usage metrics, performance data, and optionally user prompts
  • Data transmission depends on your configuration in ~/.env.cc
  • You can disable telemetry by setting CLAUDE_CODE_ENABLE_TELEMETRY=0

Users should review their telemetry configuration and understand what data is being collected and where it is being sent.
